# Prototype 5 — Cozy Exploration · Design Review

Played on: iPhone (Safari, LAN) · echo modes tested: near · surah: Al-Falaq
Status: early playtest (first device pass), notes accumulating.

1. **What emotion was this prototype optimized for?**
   Calm wonder — a flat, danger-free garden where the dawn breaks as you gather.

2. **What worked especially well?**
   - The campfire being earned - unlit and inert until every gem is found
     (playtest 2026-07-12: "I really like the campfire and how it's not
     available until you get all the gems").
   - Flowers blooming where a gem is collected ("love the blooming flowers").

3. **What distracted from memorization?**
   - **Ambient echo** (an uncollected ayah softly reciting from its direction).
     Read as *random* — no clear cause for when it fired — and collecting a gem
     mid-echo cut it off, then re-recited, which felt like a glitch. → Changed
     to **collect-triggered only**; ambient echo now defaults off (2026-07-12).

4. **Would this scale across 30–40 surahs?**
   (tbd)

5. **What should be carried forward into the final game?**
   - Recitation tied to the moment of collection (clear cause → effect).

## Controls / phone notes
- Twin walk buttons were too large and "tap right half to jump" was invisible.
  → Replaced with a left thumbstick + explicit jump button (2026-07-12).
- Tuning knobs were URL-only. → Added an in-app tuning panel (sliders icon).
