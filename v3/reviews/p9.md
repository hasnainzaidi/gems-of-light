# Prototype 9 — Open World · Design Review

Played on: (tbd) · echo mode tested: (tbd) · sessions: (tbd)

1. **What emotion was this prototype optimized for?**

   Freedom — *my own path*. The child starts in the center of a wide meadow
   that opens on both sides, and all five Ayah Gems are reachable from the
   first step, each in a different direction and with its own character (an
   easy plain-sight win east, a westward gem past a stepping-stone pond, one
   high on a climbable mound, one lofted off a bounce blossom, one tucked
   under a lone tree at the far edge). Minimal gating; the order is entirely
   the child's to choose. **Compare against p10** (the fully guided, linear
   trail) — the pair is the brief's core experiment: does order-freedom help
   or hurt memorization versus a directed path?
