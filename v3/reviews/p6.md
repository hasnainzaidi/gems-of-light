# Prototype 6 — Hidden World · Design Review

Played on: (tbd) · echo mode tested: off · surah: Al-Falaq
Status: built and reachability-verified; awaiting first device playtest.

1. **What emotion was this prototype optimized for?**
   The thrill of finding — that small, private jolt when solid-looking rock
   softens away and a chamber the world was quietly keeping opens just for you.
   The garden reads as complete from the path; curiosity is never required, only
   rewarded. Three secrets escalate the payoff: a pocket of noor seeds you can
   duck into, a gem the path walks straight through behind a curtain, and — the
   deepest secret in the game — a mesa almost everyone climbs over, its blossom
   buried in the darkest veil below. Al-Falaq, the surah of refuge in the
   hidden, learned by seeking out what is hidden.
