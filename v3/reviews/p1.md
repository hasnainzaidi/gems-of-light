# Prototype 1 — dense forest · Design Review

Played on: iPhone 16 (852×393) · echo mode tested: off · sessions: (tbd)

1. **What emotion was this prototype optimized for?**

   Curiosity — the quiet thrill of *finding*. Every gem is set off the main
   line so it reads as a small discovery rather than a handout: one crowns a
   flowered mound at the entrance, one hides up in a canopy of slabs strung
   through the leaves, one waits in a carved hollow dipped below the roots, one
   springs high off a hidden bounce clearing, and the last sits atop a stair of
   branches. Three parallel ways through the trees (forest floor, canopy lane,
   underground hollow) reward wandering over beelining, and with no hazards, no
   timers, and no direction, the only pressure is the child's own wondering
   "what's over there?" The deep-orchard `humazah` shade and dense
   olive/cypress/palm cover make the woods feel worth exploring.
