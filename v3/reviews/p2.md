# Prototype 2 — Vertical Climb · Design Review

Played on: (tbd) · echo mode tested: off · sessions: (tbd)

1. **What emotion was this prototype optimized for?**

   Earned ascent — the felt sense of *rising*. Where the cozy garden (P5) spreads
   the surah out horizontally as a stroll, the climb stacks it vertically: the
   world is a mountain (the Mountain of Light — hence the `alaq → alaqEnd` dawn
   drift), and every jump is a rung gained that you never give back. The five
   gems are placed as landmarks of height — one just off the trailhead, then the
   mid-stair, the outer shoulder, the high slab face, and the last one with the
   peak in sight — so progress is legible as altitude, not distance. The
   hypothesis under test: does a single, always-upward journey (switchback ledges,
   waterfalls pouring past, the summit visible from partway up) create a stronger
   sense of progression than wandering? The intent is that reaching the campfire
   on the summit plateau should feel like *summiting*, and that memory of the
   surah maps onto memory of the climb — each ayah pinned to a height you had to
   work to reach.
